TITLE: Remote Work & Hybrid Workplace Policy

DOCUMENT_TYPE: Policy
DEPARTMENT: Human Resources
LAST_UPDATED: 20 January 2025

SUMMARY:
Defines guidelines for hybrid work models, remote work eligibility, and employee responsibilities.

KEYWORDS:
WFH, hybrid work, remote policy, flexibility, HR

CONTENT:

## 1. Policy Overview
The organization follows a hybrid work model to balance flexibility and collaboration.

## 2. Eligibility Criteria
- Minimum 6 months tenure
- Role must support remote execution

## 3. Hybrid Work Model
- 3 days office (mandatory)
- 2 days remote

## 4. Work Expectations
- Availability during core hours
- Participation in all scheduled meetings
- Timely delivery of work

## 5. Technology & Security
- Mandatory use of VPN
- Secure handling of company data
- Use of company-issued devices only

## 6. Compliance Monitoring
- Attendance tracked via HRMS
- Performance monitored via KPIs
